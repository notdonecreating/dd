Oeuvres

Projects beyond my present priorities that I hope to make happen someday (or that someone else makes them happen!)

Digital Garden Platform
Software / Knowledge Management
An elegant, simple platform for creating and maintaining digital gardens - public spaces for organizing and sharing knowledge that grow organically over time. Focus on beautiful typography, sustainable long-term use, and tools for connecting ideas across entries.
Analog Social Media
Social / Hardware
A physical device that allows people to share updates, photos, and messages with loved ones without screens or traditional social media. Think digital picture frame meets private newsletter, but with intentionally limited features to avoid addictive patterns.
Wonder Education Framework
Education / Curriculum
A comprehensive approach to education that centers wonder, curiosity, and true understanding rather than memorization or test performance. Developing materials and training for educators who want to preserve the innate joy of learning children are born with.
Third Space Community Centers
Community / Architecture
A network of thoughtfully designed physical spaces that serve as neither home nor work, but as true "third places" where community can form naturally. Combining elements of libraries, cafes, makerspaces, and parks into spaces that foster meaningful connections.
Low-Tech Luxury
Consumer Products / Design
A brand creating high-quality, beautiful products that intentionally eschew "smart" technology in favor of durability, repairability, and timeless design. Reframing the absence of technology as a feature rather than a limitation.
