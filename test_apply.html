Test Form - Direct Submission

Important: This is a direct form submission test. After submitting, you will be redirected to Google Forms confirmation page, then back to this page.

Troubleshooting Steps:

  1. This form uses a direct POST submission to Google Forms, which will redirect you to Google's confirmation page.
  2. After submission, check your Google Form's response tab to see if this test submission is recorded.
  3. If this test form works but your main form doesn't, the issue is with your form submission method.
  4. If this test form also doesn't work, the issue might be with your Google Form configuration.

Check Google Form Settings:

  1. Make sure your Google Form is open to accepting responses
  2. Verify the spreadsheet is properly linked to your form
  3. Check if there might be form settings limiting submissions
